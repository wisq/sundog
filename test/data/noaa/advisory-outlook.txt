SPACE WEATHER ADVISORY OUTLOOK #17-36
2017 September 3 at 11:52 p.m. MDT (2017 September 4 0552 UTC)

**** SPACE WEATHER OUTLOOK ****

Summary For August 28-September 3

G1 (Minor) geomagnetic storm levels were observed on 31 August and 02 September due to coronal hole high speed stream effects.

Outlook For September 4-10

There is a chance for R1-R2 (Minor-Moderate) radio blackouts for 04-10 September due to potential significant flare activity from active sunspot Regions 2673 and 2674.
